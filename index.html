Okay, but how do I make it?

You just need coffee beans + a coffee grinder + a filter cone + paper filters.

Steps:

  1. Put on a kettle.
  2. Grind coffee beans.
  3. Place a filter in the filter cone.
  4. Scoop coffee grounds into filter. (~1-2 T per 6 oz as a guide but you'll find your own perfect recipe.)
  5. Pour hot water over filter into mug/glass of your choice.
  6. When coffee has reached your level and strength of preference, sip up.
  7. Say good morning to your plants and smile.
